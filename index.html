Hello, this is Tianna Doan

Tianna Doan is a Visual Communication Design student at San Francisco State University. She was previously a student at City College of San Francisco, experimenting with multiple paths. On May 23rd, 2024, Tianna graduated with an associate’s degree in liberal arts after three years and a semester.

Tianna is an aspiring illustrator and designer. She has always been an imaginative and creative person at a young age, often conceptualizing stories in her head. Although she drew a lot, Tianna finally gained more confidence as an artist in 2020, when she began sharing more of her artwork and characters.

Tianna is very passionate about her characters and stories. Her stories are all part of a connected universe, with themes focusing on magic, coming-of-age, and self-discovery. One day, she hopes to share their stories, whether it’s through illustrations, comic strips, or even an animated series.
